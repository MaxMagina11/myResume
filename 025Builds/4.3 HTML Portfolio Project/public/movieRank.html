ANIME RECOMMENDATIONS

MY TOP 3 LIST

  • One Piece

    •

One Piece is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. It has been serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine since July 22, 1997, and has been collected into 96 tankōbon volumes as of April 2020. The story follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy.

  • Kaiju No.8

    •

Kaiju No.8 is a Japanese manga series written by Naoya Matsumoto. It has been serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine since July 22, 2020. The story follows the adventures of Kafka Hibino.

  • Attack on Titan

    •

Attack on Titan is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Hajime Isayama, The story follows Eren Yeager, his adoptive sister Mikasa Ackerman, and their friend Armin Arlert, who join the military after their hometown is destroyed and Eren's mother is killed by Titans. They strive to fight back and uncover the mysteries behind the Titans, the walls, and the world beyond.
